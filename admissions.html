Admissions

We welcome new students to join our vibrant community! Applications are open for all grades.

How to Apply

  1. Download the admission form.
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, transcripts, photos).
  3. Attend an interview and assessment session.

Important Dates

Applications open from January 15 to March 30 every year.
